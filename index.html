What is Mastermind?

Mastermind or Master Mind is a code-breaking game for two players. It resembles an earlier pencil and paper game called Bulls and Cows that may date back a century.

About the Game

The game has several variations, but the basic rules are the same. Each turn, one player (codemaker) sets a secret code and the other player (codebreaker) tries to guess it. According to the accuracy of these guesses, the codemaker gives feedback. When the secret code is solved, the round ends and scoring is done.

How to Play?

  1. Pick a color from the color board on the right and drop it into the guessing area at the bottom of the game board. Secret code can contain the same color multiple times, but not spaces. You can change the colors you choose by repicking again before confirming your guess.
  2. Make your guess with Play button
  3. Evaluate the Feedback
    The codemaker will evaluate the pattern you gave in terms of order and color and will give you a feedback here. A peg of correct order and color is represented by a small red peg. However a small white peg indicates the existence of a correct color code peg placed in the wrong position. The location of the pegs in this feedback is random.
  4. Win the Game!
    Four red pegs means you win the game. Game has originally no hint system so you don't have to use it. You can challenge with yourself in terms of time and number of guesses. Guessing a number of five or less is better than most algorithms.
